# Convert PDF to Excel - Quick Guide

Your PDF file: `Pre-Foreclosure list pulled Dec 10 2025.pdf`

## Easiest Method (Recommended)

### Option 1: Adobe Online Converter (Free)
1. Go to: https://www.adobe.com/acrobat/online/pdf-to-excel.html
2. Click "Select a file"
3. Choose: `Pre-Foreclosure list pulled Dec 10 2025.pdf`
4. Click "Convert to Excel"
5. Download the Excel file
6. Upload it to your app!

### Option 2: ILovePDF (Free)
1. Go to: https://www.ilovepdf.com/pdf-to-excel
2. Upload your PDF
3. Click "Convert to Excel"
4. Download and upload to your app

### Option 3: Microsoft Excel (If you have it)
1. Open Excel
2. File → Open → Select your PDF
3. Excel will convert it automatically
4. Save as .xlsx

## After Conversion

Once you have the Excel file:
1. Rename it to: `Pre-Foreclosure list pulled Dec 10 2025.xlsx`
2. Upload it through your app's upload interface
3. The app will process it automatically!

## Note

The app now supports both PDF and Excel files, but for the best results with complex PDFs, converting to Excel first gives you more control over the data structure.
